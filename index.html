About me:

I am a thirty-one years guy with a bachelor degree in installation design and a master degree in modernising the energy systems within buildings’ environment.
Well, when it comes to ‘modernising’ topic, it’s obviously that ‘tech’ word should be included. This is how came the idea of studing Front-End Development.
I think that the future belongs to those who never stop exploring new ideas of doing the things faster, easier and more intelligent.
Being an installation design engineer it doesn't seem a big deal, but can you imagine a building without installations? Can you live in a house without water , gas , electricity and sewerage?

Hobbies:

During my free time I like to spend time in nature. So, in the weekends I often organize hiking tours near Brasov, on Piatra Mare, Postavaru, Piatra Craiului, Bucegi etc.
When the weather is not very friendly, I like to watch movies, go to the theatre or different events.
Sometimes I run competitions not to win but for the taste of fresh air and that good vibe which comes together with a competition.
The newest hobby and purpose is growing this site and populating it with quality content.
